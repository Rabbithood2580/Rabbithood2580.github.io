Hi, I'm Gayoung!

I am a M.S. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Seoul National University advised by professor Shenghui Cui. Currently, I am researching optimization of modulation and transformer design of dual-active bridge converters for high-efficiency EV DC fast charging. I am grateful to be supported by Domestic Graduate Scholarships of SBS Foundation. My research interests include data center power supplies, renewable integration and transportation electrification.

Outside of research, I love traveling around, taking photographs, and playing a guitar.
